h1. Happiness Index

Happiness is what we want to achieve. We understand that what makes you happy doesn't necessarily make me happy. So in the future we probably have to agree on some common criteria. 

As we're still a small team. Measuring our happiness is as simple as asking it during "weekly Hangouts call":communication.html. For the moment this way of working suffices to rate our general happiness.
